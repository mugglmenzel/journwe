Get Your Trip Started

  • Where do you want to go?

You haven't decided yet? - Doesn't matter, start anyway and discuss later.

Involve Your Friends

Use JournWe to motivate friends to join an adventure. Sharing information from JournWe is a cool way for having all your friends convinced to join your trip! Or announce it to the public and fiend new friends.

Keep it all in One Place

Don't bother with emails from travel operators that only reach one or few people in your travel group. JournWe gives you an email address (your-adventure@@journwe.com) and provides you with a single communication point. All of it is kept centrally in your adventure visible for all travel mates.

Get Inspired

Is there anything out there? Find the hottest current adventures you may join. We have great inspirations for you to find your next adventure! Activate a notifier and get news on adventures in your area, a certain category or on where you always wanted to go.

Find the Cheapest Prices

Use reference booking systems to find cheapest flights, hotels, buses, trains, homestays, tents, backseats,tickets... matching your trip.

And when it's Over
